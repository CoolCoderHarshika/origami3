Origiamis

Here are some tutorials to make Origiamis

Chamelion origami Step 1) Fold and unfold the paper in half both ways. Then fold the top and bottom to the centre. Step 2) Fold and unfold both sides to the centre. Step 3) Fold all the corners down along the diagonal dotted lines

Camel Origami Step 1) Fold and Unfold the sides and the top along the dotted lines. You'll use these creases in the next step. Step 2) Open up the top layer of paper and fold it up using the crease from the previous step. Step 3) Fold in both sides along the existing creases making a Petal Fold
